What “pregenerated” should mean here

I think we should define this carefully.

There are three pregeneration levels:

Level 1: pregenerated metadata

Best option.

Level 2: pregenerated frame data

Frames are computed ahead of time and stored as arrays or compressed text.
Possible, but likely bloated and harder to edit.

Level 3: pregenerated videos/GIFs

Playable assets only.
Least flexible.

I recommend Level 1.
